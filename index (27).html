Vanessa Dion Fletcher
BIG Doll || Xwat Naaniitus
A Lenape and Potawatomi neurodiverse Artist working in performance, textiles, and video. Employs porcupine quills, Wampum belts, and menstrual blood to reveal the complexities of what defines a body physically and culturally. MFA, School of the Art Institute of Chicago, 2016.
Kelsey Pugh
Reconstructing Ancient Faces
Paleoanthropologist studying ape and human evolution from the fossil record. Uses comparative anatomy, phylogenetics, and 3D geometric morphometrics. PhD in Biological Anthropology, City University of New York, 2020.
Anna Stielau
Arts of Darkness
Assistant professor of art history and visual culture at OCAD University. PhD, NYU Department of Media, Culture, and Communication. Weisman Postdoctoral Teaching Fellow in Visual Culture, Caltech. Research explores how contemporary African artists use media and technology.
